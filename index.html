Step 1

Greeting

Rules:
  • Be professional and polite
  • Respond within first 30 seconds
  • Always include company name

"Hello! Thank you for contacting us. My name is [Your Name]. How can I assist you today?"

Step 2

Verify Customer

Rules:
  • Always verify before accessing account
  • Request account number or email
  • Confirm security questions if needed

"To better assist you, could you please provide me with your account number or email address associated with your account?"

Step 3

Listen & Understand

Rules:
  • Do NOT interrupt the customer
  • Take notes of the issue
  • Confirm you understand the problem

"I understand. Let me make sure I have this right: [Summarize the issue]. Is that correct?"

Step 4

Provide Solution

Rules:
  • Provide clear, step-by-step solutions
  • Do NOT promise what you can't deliver
  • Escalate if needed

"Here's what I can do to help: [Provide solution]. Would this work for you?"

Step 5

Closing

Rules:
  • Always thank the customer
  • Confirm next steps
  • Offer further assistance

"Thank you so much for contacting us. Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

✅ All scripts follow customer service best practices
